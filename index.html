Abstract

Customer churn is a major problem in the telecom industry. Traditional models often fail due to class imbalance and inability to capture subtle patterns. We propose an Ensemble Stacking Model combining Random Forest, KNN, and Naïve Bayes, fed into a Logistic Regression meta-learner. Our model achieves state-of-the-art performance while introducing the Latency Aware Accuracy Index (LAAI) for real-world efficiency.
